⌥ Open Science · advocacy

An open practice.

“I am an avid advocate for Open Science.” “Together, we can do better!”

Open Science is the practice of doing research in the open — data, code, methods, and reasoning shared so that the field can audit and build on what we publish. Three strands of the work: ODIN, the community I help convene around open neurophysiology data; essays, where I argue for the practice; and papers, where I work the argument out.
